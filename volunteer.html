Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to volunteer?

No prior experience is necessary for most positions. We provide training and ongoing support to all our volunteers.

What is the time commitment required?

Time commitments vary by role. Some positions require just a few hours per week, while others might need more regular involvement. We're happy to work with your schedule.

Are there age restrictions for volunteering?

Volunteers must be at least 16 years old. Some positions may have higher age requirements depending on the nature of the work.

Will I receive training?

Yes, all volunteers receive comprehensive training relevant to their role. We also provide ongoing support and development opportunities.
